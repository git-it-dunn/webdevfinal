How to play the game:
You will try to guess the randomly chosen word in as little tries as possible. You will guess the random word letter by letter. To guess a letter within the word, select the letter from the table below then click where you think it will go in the word in one of the four squares. If you get the guessed letter wrong, your incorrect guess number will increase, if you get over 20 you lose. If you can get all four letters right, you win. Click anywhere off this popup to play!
